Company History

Accessories and Supplies Depot, Inc. was incorporated March 1999. Our goal is to provide our clientele with the best product and excellent service at a cost-effective price. During our early years, the company also known to many as ASDI (as-dee) only sold computer accessories to various retail outfits in various malls in Metro Manila.

A few years after, our company expanded our product portfolio by selling computer consumables such as ink cartridges, toner cartridges and consumer and commercial printers.

While we have managed to independently expand our operation in Metro Manila and also outside, specifically in the northern and southern Luzon are, and in the key cities of Visayas and Mindanao, HP Inc. took notice of our strength when it comes to the availability of various supplies, nationwide distribution and consumer reach with these IT products. This led for doors to open for the company. Our relationship and ties with HP Inc., further strengthened by our appointment as one of their authorized sub-distributor. Epson on the other hand, elevated us as their Platinum Partner from Gold, which is attributed to our excellent track record and consistent year-on-year growth and target achievement. We are also one of the few selected in the Canon Elite Circle of Canon Marketing Philippines.
